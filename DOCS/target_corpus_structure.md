# 📊 Corpus Summary

* **18 legal domains**
* **~155 primary legal sources** (Acts, Codes, Rules, Regulations)
* **8 categories of secondary legal materials** (judgments, circulars, notifications, reports)
* Coverage spans constitutional, criminal, civil, commercial, corporate, taxation, cyber, labour, consumer, banking, healthcare, environmental, education, agriculture, housing, and judicial precedents, providing a strong foundation for a production-grade Indian legal RAG system.
